What to do in Pergamum, Turkey!

All about Pergamum

Pergamum, also known as Bergama, was an ancient Greek City. It is located very close to the beautiful Aegean Sea. Today is is the modern city of Bergama. The city of Pergamum can be traced all the way back to the 5th century BCE. Today many tourists come to see attractions such as the Pergamum Altar, the Asklepion and the Akropolis.Many tourists describe Pergamum as fine ancient city with scenic views.
